About the Distinguished Scholarships Program

The WSU Distinguished Scholarships Program (DSP) works with more than 400 students each year as they imagine, create, and define their own transformational experience at Washington State University. Specifically, our office provides one-to-one mentoring with students across the WSU system as they develop the skills to apply for highly prestigious awards (Rhodes, Fulbright, Marshall, Truman, Gates Cambridge, etc.). These awards are used to help finance distinctive educational experiences at one’s home university, or at renowned institutions abroad. Many high-achieving and goal-oriented students begin preparing as early as their freshman year.
