From the Director

Dear WSU Students,

Thank you for visiting the Distinguished Scholarships page! Our office is designed to help Washington State University students and alumni broaden their educations with significant, prestigious, and nationally competitive awards. Being a strong student is just one step in your progression as a scholar. My goal is to help you develop skills that integrate your academic, personal development, and career goals through the self-reflective process of submitting an application for a distinguished scholarship.

I encourage you to review the award links, consider the experiences you have (or wish to have) and schedule an appointment with me (over the phone, via Skype or in person) to talk about what makes YOU the best candidate for recognition on the national stage. I look forward to talking with you soon!

April Seehafer, M.Ed.
Director of the Distinguished Scholarships Program
