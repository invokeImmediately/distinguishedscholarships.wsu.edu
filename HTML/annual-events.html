Annual Events

of the WSU Distinguished Scholarships Program

Each fall, winter, and spring, the WSU Distinguished Scholarships Program hosts annual events focused on the prestigious, nationally competitive awards that provide great educational experiences for our students and alumni.

Celebrate Distinguished Scholars

This annual, fall networking event is designed to celebrate all WSU students who received a distinguished scholarship that year. Each awardee is recognized and given a unique WSU Distinguished Scholarships pin. The names of all awardees are included in the informational, permanent display in the WSU Distinguished Scholarships Gallery in the Compton Union Building, the site of this annual event.

Freshman Scholars Progression

This invitation-only, mid-winter event is to inspire high-achieving freshmen to consider pursuing prestigious, nationally competitive awards to broaden their educational experience and boost their résumés.

At each annual Progression in January:

  • Students who earned a top GPA in fall semester are recognized for this achievement.
  • Top faculty and staff members explain to tables of students what steps can be taken along the paths to distinguished scholarships.

Distinguished Scholarships Program Reception

This spring event is to honor the year’s distinguished scholarship recipients and applicants as well as their faculty and staff mentors. The celebration is held each year in the WSU Distinguished Scholarships Gallery in the lower level of the CUB.
