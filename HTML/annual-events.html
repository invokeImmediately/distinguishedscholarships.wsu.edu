Annual Events

of the WSU Distinguished Scholarships Program

Each fall, winter, and spring, the WSU Distinguished Scholarships Program hosts annual events focused on our students and alumni who seek prestigious, nationally competitive awards that provide great educational experiences.

Events List

Distinguished Scholars Celebration

The entire university community is invited to this annual, fall networking event to celebrate all WSU students who received a distinguished scholarship that year. Each awardee is recognized and given a unique WSU Distinguished Scholar pin. The names of all awardees are included in the informational, permanent display in the WSU Distinguished Scholars Gallery in the Compton Union Building, the site of this annual event. The first Distinguished Scholars Celebration was held in 2014 and coincided with the dedication of the Distinguished Scholars Gallery. The next Celebration is set for Oct. 19, 2017.

Freshman Scholars Progression

This invitation-only, mid-winter event is to inspire high-achieving freshmen to consider pursuing prestigious, nationally competitive awards to broaden their educational experience and boost their résumés. The next Progression is set for Jan. 26, 2018.

At each annual Progression in January:

  • Students who earned a top GPA in fall semester are recognized for this achievement.
  • Top faculty and staff members explain to tables of students what steps can be taken along the paths to distinguished scholarships.

Distinguished Scholars Reception

This spring event is to honor the year’s distinguished scholarship applicants as well as their faculty and staff mentors. The celebration is held each year in CUE 518.

Details for the next Distinguished Scholars Reception:
Date: Friday, March 2, 2018
Time: 12:00 – 1:00 p.m.
Location: CUE 518

About Distinguished Scholarships

“Distinguished scholarships” are prestigious, nationally competitive awards including grants, scholarships, and fellowships for students, graduates, faculty and staff members, and others, used to help finance distinctive educational experiences at one’s home university, or at renowned universities abroad.

The WSU Distinguished Scholarships Program:

  • Provides unique support services for all scholars
  • Coordinates preparation of candidates for prestigious, nationally competitive awards
  • Authorizes WSU endorsement letters
  • Hosts three distinctive events each year related to distinguished scholarships
  • Is a key member of WSU Undergraduate Education programming
