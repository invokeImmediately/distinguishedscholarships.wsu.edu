You can follow the link above to find out more about the workshops appearing in the calendar below, which are provided by the Distinguished Scholarships Program to help build your eligibility for prestigious national awards. Furthermore, by clicking on any of the events appearing in the calendar, your browser will navigate to that event's detailed description. Calendar days during which a workshop will be hosted are highlighted in different colors (see legend), and unless otherwise indicated, workshops are held in CUE 512.
