Other Award Opportunities

For DACA Students

Just as WSU is dedicated to providing unqualified support to our undocumented students, there are scholarship organizations that value equal access to affordable higher education and welcome applications from undocumented students.

Scholarships Open to Undocumented Students
United We Dream has prepared this list of 10 scholarship resources open for undocumented students. The list includes both awards for which undocumented students are eligible as well as scholarship databases specifically designed to assist undocumented students in finding award opportunities.

Educators for Fair Consideration (E4FC)
E4FC maintains a list of scholarships available for undocumented students that don't have U.S. citizenship or legal permanent residency. Note that some of the listed opportunities have regional eligibility requirements that make WSU students ineligible for that scholarship; however, many of the scholarships do not have such requirements and are open to WSU students.
