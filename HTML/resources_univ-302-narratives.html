Univ 302: Putting Myself on Paper

Learn how to stand out from the crowd. This carefully designed course is led by experts in composition who know what reviewers look for in successful applications. UNIV 302 instructors will guide you to recognize your strengths and interests, and lead hands-on exercises so you can put those thoughts into well-crafted, winning essays.

Course Details for Summer Session 2016

Course number: 7569 • Section: 6 • Weekly schedule: Tu & Thu, 12:10 - 1 p.m. • Location: CUE 402 • Length of course: May 9 - June 17 (six weeks) • Grading: pass/fail • Number of credits: 1

Why This Course Is Helpful

Because it helps you compete with the best and the brightest. If you are planning to apply for a prestigious, nationally competitive scholarships, you will have to submit a strong, well-written composition on what you’ve accomplished and what you aspire to do. So do hundreds of other applicants. This course will teach you how to make your essay stand out.
