Univ 303: Strategies for Writing Personal Statements

Learn how to stand out from the crowd. This carefully designed course is led by experts in composition who know what reviewers look for in successful applications. UNIV 303 instructors will guide you to recognize your strengths and interests, and lead hands-on exercises so you can put those thoughts into well-crafted, winning essays.

Course Details for Fall 2016

Full course title: Compositing and Evaluation Strategies • Weekly schedule: Wed, 12:10 - 1 p.m. • Location: CUE 303 • Number of credits: 1

Why This Course Is Helpful

Because it helps you compete with the best and the brightest. If you are planning to apply for a prestigious, nationally competitive scholarships, you will have to submit a strong, well-written composition on what you’ve accomplished and what you aspire to do. So do hundreds of other applicants. This course will teach you how to make your essay stand out.
